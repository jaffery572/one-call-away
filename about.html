2012

Humble Beginnings

Founded by Jack Wilson, a former restaurant owner who saw the need for specialized call center services for food businesses. Started with 5 agents serving local restaurants in New York.

2015

Expansion & Specialization

Expanded to 25 agents and introduced specialized training programs for food industry terminology and customer service protocols. Launched our home delivery support service.

2018

Technology Integration

Invested in advanced call center technology and introduced integration with popular POS systems and delivery platforms. Reached 100+ client milestone.

2020

Pandemic Response

Rapidly adapted services to support restaurants during COVID-19, helping them transition to delivery and takeout models. Added virtual assistant and analytics services.

2023

Industry Leadership

Now serving 200+ food businesses across North America with a team of 50+ specialized agents. Recognized as a leader in food industry call center solutions.
