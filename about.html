Our Story

Founded in 2020, UK Call Center was born from a simple observation: UK takeaways and restaurants were struggling with inconsistent customer service, missed orders, and overwhelmed staff during peak hours.

We started with a small team of 5 agents serving local takeaways in London. Today, we handle over 50,000 calls monthly for food businesses across the United Kingdom.
